Homemade Chili

Chili

This is my version of the chili I grew up with, with all the changes that I wanted to make from mixes of other chili recipes. Serves 8-12

  • 1 lbs of ground beef
  • 1 lbs of ground turkey
  • 1 14oz. can of kidney beans, rinsed
  • 1 14oz. can or black beans, rinsed
  • 1 14oz. can of fire roasted crushed tomatoes
  • 1 14oz. can of fire roasted diced tomatoes
  • 1 medium green pepper, diced
  • 2-4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 large white onion, diced
  • 1/2 a bag of frozen corn
  • 2 medium fresh tomatoes, diced
  • 2 Tbsp of ground coffee, medium or dark roast
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Chili Powder
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Inca Fire Salt
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 2oz. tequila or 1/2 can of beer

Ingredients

  1. In a large cast iron pot, cook the ground beef and ground turkey.
  2. Season the meat with the seasonings
  3. Add the Garlic and cook until lightly browned
